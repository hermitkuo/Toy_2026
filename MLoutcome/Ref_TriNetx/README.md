## A. CKD 藥物治療：MRA / Finerenone

### 1. Finerenone vs. spironolactone in CKD and type 2 diabetes: a target trial emulation
- **作者**：Wang CA, et al. (8 authors)
- **期刊**：*Nature Communications*, 2025 Oct
- **DOI**：[10.1038/s41467-025-64640-3](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-64640-3) ｜ **PMID**: 41173939 ｜ [PMC12579246](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12579246/)
- **重點**：以目標試驗模擬（target trial emulation）比較 finerenone 與傳統 spironolactone 在 CKD 合併第二型糖尿病病人之心腎結局，回應臨床上類固醇 vs. 非類固醇 MRA 選擇的關鍵問題。頂尖綜合期刊。

### 2. Cardio-kidney outcomes for combined vs. monotherapy with finerenone or SGLT2 inhibitors in CKD
- **作者**：Chuang MH, et al. (8 authors) — Chi Mei Medical Center, Taiwan
- **期刊**：*Nephrology Dialysis Transplantation (NDT)*, 2025 Sep
- **DOI**：[10.1093/ndt/gfaf064](https://doi.org/10.1093/ndt/gfaf064) ｜ **PMID**: 40199739 ｜ [PMC12477464](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12477464/)
- **重點**：探討 finerenone 與 SGLT2i 「合併治療」相較於單藥之心腎保護效益，直接切中目前 CKD 多支柱（pillar）治療策略的熱門議題。歐洲腎臟醫學會旗艦期刊。

### 3. Non-steroidal vs. steroidal MRA: cardiovascular outcomes and hyperkalemia risk in DM + CKD + HF
- **作者**：Okunlola AO, et al. (8 authors)
- **期刊**：*Cureus*, 2025 Nov
- **DOI**：[10.7759/cureus.97588](https://doi.org/10.7759/cureus.97588) ｜ **PMID**: 41293605 ｜ [PMC12641577](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12641577/)
- **重點**：在糖尿病、CKD 與心衰竭三重共病族群中，比較非類固醇與類固醇 MRA 的心血管結局與高血鉀風險，對臨床用藥安全性具參考價值。

## B. CKD 藥物治療：SGLT2i / GLP-1 受體促效劑

### 4. GLP-1 RA in type 2 diabetes and advanced CKD: kidney and cardiovascular outcomes
- **作者**：Hsiao CC, et al. (7 authors) — Chang Gung, Taiwan
- **期刊**：*Clinical Kidney Journal (CKJ)*, 2025 May
- **DOI**：[10.1093/ckj/sfaf172](https://doi.org/10.1093/ckj/sfaf172) ｜ **PMID**: 40510690 ｜ [PMC12160804](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12160804/)
- **重點**：聚焦「晚期 CKD」族群（臨床試驗常排除）使用 GLP-1 RA 的真實世界腎臟與心血管結局，補足實證缺口。

### 5. Cardiovascular and kidney outcomes of GLP-1 RA in adults with obesity: a target trial emulation
- **作者**：Tang H, et al. (9 authors) — University of Pennsylvania (CHASE)
- **期刊**：*Diabetes, Obesity & Metabolism*, 2025 Aug
- **DOI**：[10.1111/dom.70054](https://doi.org/10.1111/dom.70054) ｜ **PMID**: 40874398 ｜ [PMC12515787](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12515787/)
- **重點**：在「肥胖（不限糖尿病）」成人以目標試驗模擬評估 GLP-1 RA 的心腎結局，方法學嚴謹。

### 6. Effectiveness of semaglutide on survival in type 2 diabetes and CKD
- **作者**：Kishimori T, et al. (10 authors)
- **期刊**：*Open Heart*, 2025 Jul
- **DOI**：[10.1136/openhrt-2025-003382](https://doi.org/10.1136/openhrt-2025-003382) ｜ **PMID**: 40628673 ｜ [PMC12243591](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12243591/)（CC BY-NC）
- **重點**：評估 semaglutide 在 T2D 合併 CKD 病人之存活結局，呼應 FLOW 試驗後的真實世界驗證。

### 7. GLP-1 RA in type 2 diabetic kidney transplant recipients: cardiovascular and kidney outcomes
- **作者**：Lin LC, et al. (4 authors) — National Taiwan University Hospital
- **期刊**：*Cardiovascular Diabetology*, 2025 Feb
- **DOI**：[10.1186/s12933-025-02649-0](https://doi.org/10.1186/s12933-025-02649-0) ｜ **PMID**: 39984953 ｜ [PMC11846168](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11846168/)
- **重點**：針對糖尿病腎臟移植受者此一特殊且資料稀缺的族群，評估 GLP-1 RA 的心腎效益。

## C. CKD 貧血、礦物質骨病變與透析

### 8. HIF-PHI vs. ESA on prognosis in non-dialysis CKD: a propensity-matched cohort
- **作者**：Hou YC, et al. (9 authors) — Cardinal Tien Hospital / Fu Jen, Taiwan
- **期刊**：*Renal Failure*, 2025 Dec
- **DOI**：[10.1080/0886022X.2025.2592442](https://doi.org/10.1080/0886022X.2025.2592442) ｜ **PMID**: 41344335 ｜ [PMC12679846](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12679846/)（CC BY 4.0）
- **重點**：比較缺氧誘導因子脯胺酸羥化酶抑制劑（HIF-PHI, 如 roxadustat）與傳統紅血球生成刺激劑（ESA）在非透析 CKD 貧血之預後，為當前 CKD 貧血治療轉換的核心議題。

### 9. Vitamin D deficiency and clinical outcomes of calcimimetic therapy in dialysis patients
- **作者**：Lu KC, et al. (6 authors) — Taipei Tzu Chi Hospital, Taiwan
- **期刊**：*Nutrients*, 2025 Apr
- **DOI**：[10.3390/nu17091536](https://doi.org/10.3390/nu17091536) ｜ **PMID**: 40362848 ｜ [PMC12073363](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12073363/)（CC BY 4.0）
- **重點**：探討維生素 D 缺乏對透析病人 calcimimetic（擬鈣劑）治療結局之影響，與 CKD-MBD（礦物質骨病變）次發性副甲狀腺亢進管理相關。

## E. 腎臟結局之風險因子與預測模型

### 11. ESKD, major cardiovascular events, and death associated with systemic inflammation
- **作者**：Halimi JM, et al. (7 authors) — CHU de Tours, France
- **期刊**：*Kidney International Reports*, 2026 Feb
- **DOI**：[10.1016/j.ekir.2026.106364](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ekir.2026.106364) ｜ **PMID**: 41853745 ｜ [PMC12995490](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12995490/)
- **重點**：探討全身性發炎與末期腎病（ESKD）、重大心血管事件及死亡之關聯。國際腎臟醫學會（ISN）旗下全 Open Access 期刊。

### 12. Risk of hypertension and CKD following aldosterone dysregulation
- **作者**：Townsend RR, et al. (7 authors) — University of Pennsylvania (Renal Medicine)
- **期刊**：*American Journal of Hypertension*, 2026 Jan
- **DOI**：[10.1093/ajh/hpaf183](https://doi.org/10.1093/ajh/hpaf183) ｜ **PMID**: 40929018 ｜ [PMC12802914](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12802914/)
- **重點**：分析醛固酮失調（aldosterone dysregulation）後續發生高血壓與 CKD 的風險，連結內分泌性高血壓與腎臟病進展。

### 13. Major adverse kidney events among CKD patients with vitamin D deficiency
- **作者**：Lin YM, et al. (8 authors) — Chi Mei Medical Centre, Taiwan
- **期刊**：*Frontiers in Nutrition*, 2025 Oct
- **DOI**：[10.3389/fnut.2025.1650514](https://doi.org/10.3389/fnut.2025.1650514) ｜ **PMID**: 41127093 ｜ [PMC12537428](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12537428/)
- **重點**：評估維生素 D 缺乏與 CKD 病人重大不良腎臟事件（MAKE）之關聯，探討可介入之危險因子。

### 14. Testosterone therapy and reduced risk of AKI, kidney failure with RRT, and CV events in men with diabetes and hypogonadism
- **作者**：Bonnet F, et al. (7 authors) — CHU de Rennes, France
- **期刊**：*Cardiovascular Diabetology*, 2025 Oct
- **DOI**：[10.1186/s12933-025-02930-2](https://doi.org/10.1186/s12933-025-02930-2) ｜ **PMID**: 41035033 ｜ [PMC12487499](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12487499/)
- **重點**：在糖尿病合併性腺低下男性中，分析睪固酮治療與急性腎損傷、需腎臟替代治療之腎衰竭及心血管事件風險的關聯。

### 15. Machine-learning prediction of acute and chronic kidney diseases in the post-COVID-19 era (US national EHR)
- **作者**：Zhang Y, et al. (5 authors) — Penn State College of Medicine
- **期刊**：*EBioMedicine*, 2025 Apr
- **DOI**：[10.1016/j.ebiom.2025.105726](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ebiom.2025.105726) ｜ **PMID**: 40288236 ｜ [PMC12056805](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12056805/)
- **重點**：運用全美電子病歷以機器學習模型預測 AKI 與 CKD 風險，結合 ML 方法與腎臟流行病學，與 MLoutcome 主題高度契合。
